DIY Corner

Breathe new life into old garments. Upcycling is not just sustainable; it's a form of personal expression.

Featured Tutorial:
The Lace Trim Upgrade

Transform a simple slip dress or camisole by adding delicate vintage lace trim to the hem or neckline.

01

Source Your Lace

Find vintage lace at charity shops or haberdasheries. Wash and iron it flat.

02

Pin & Baste

Pin the lace along the desired edge. Use a loose basting stitch by hand to secure it temporarily.

03

Sew Securely

Using a sewing machine (or careful hand backstitch), sew along the edge. Remove basting stitches.
